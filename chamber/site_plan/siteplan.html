Site Plan: Aso-Ebi Thread

A vendor and style guide platform focused on Nigerian traditional wedding fabrics and attire.

Site Name

Aso-Ebi Thread

Reason Selected: This name is highly culturally relevant and descriptive. "Aso-Ebi" (family cloth) is the core traditional concept in Nigeria, immediately identifying the site's niche. "Thread" symbolizes both the fabric used and the connection/community the platform provides. It is short, memorable, and directly communicates the focus on Nigerian traditional fashion and coordination.

Optional Domain: asoebithread.com.ng

Site Purpose

The site serves as a comprehensive resource hub for Nigerian event guests and planners, specializing in Aso-Ebi (traditional fabric) coordination and style. It provides a curated directory of verified fabric vendors and professional tailors/designers, high-quality style galleries for inspiration, and practical tools to facilitate guests' fabric purchasing and coordination for specific events. Its primary goal is to simplify the process of styling for Nigerian traditional ceremonies while promoting quality and local vendor excellence.

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

These scenarios reflect typical challenges faced by guests and organizers of Nigerian events.

  • Scenario 1 (Guest): "I was given an Aso-Ebi fabric by the couple. Where can I find a trusted tailor in Lagos who specializes in creative, modern Yoruba wedding attire designs?"
  • Scenario 2 (Planner/Couple): "We need to purchase 150 yards of quality lace and George fabric in deep red and gold. Which certified vendor on this site offers bulk discounts and nationwide delivery?"
  • Scenario 3 (Guest): "I need style inspiration for a contemporary iro and buba style for a December wedding. Can I browse galleries filtered by 'Ankara' and 'Simple Elegant'?"

Color Scheme

The palette is inspired by the rich colours of Nigerian traditional attire, evoking celebration, royalty, and warmth.

  • Primary Brand Color (Deep Red): (Deep Maroon - #8B0000)

    Used for: Main Headings (H1, H2), Primary Buttons (e.g., 'Find Vendor'), Logo, and Highlighting Important Information.

  • Primary Accent (Gold): (Metallic Gold - #D4AF37)

    Used for: Borders, Navigation Highlight/Hover States, 'Trusted Vendor' Badges, and Secondary CTA buttons.

  • Background (Soft Beige): (Soft Beige - #F8F4E3)

    Used for: Main Body Background (provides a neutral, elegant backdrop for colourful fabrics).

  • Body Text (Dark Charcoal): (Dark Charcoal - #2D2D2D)

    Used for: All Body Paragraphs and Standard Text (ensures high contrast and readability).

Typography

The fonts mix contemporary elegance with modern readability to reflect the fashion and quality of the site.

  • Header Font:'Playfair Display', serif (Fallback: any serif).

    Used for: All Headings (H1, H2, H3). This font has an elegant, classic feel often used in high-end fashion and lifestyle publications.

  • Body Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif (Fallback: any sans-serif).

    Used for: All Body Text, Paragraphs, Buttons, and Navigation. A clean, geometric, highly readable modern font ideal for web content and mobile screens.
