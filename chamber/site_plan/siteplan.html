Aso-Ebi Thread Site Plan

Matthew Osaruyi Eruogun | WDD231

Site Name & Purpose

Name: Aso-Ebi Thread

Reasoning: The name was selected to combine the cultural significance of "Aso-Ebi" (family cloth) with the concept of a "Thread," representing both the physical sewing of garments and the chronological tracking of wedding expenses.

Purpose: To simplify the process of styling for Nigerian traditional ceremonies by providing a budget tracker and style inspiration hub. The site focuses on empowering guests to manage fashion costs privately using modern web APIs.

Scenarios

  • The Budget Conscious Guest: "How can I track my spending on lace and tailors for three different weddings this quarter so I don't exceed my budget?"
  • The Style Seeker: "I have my fabric but no design; where can I see trending Iro and Buba styles that fit within my tailoring price range?"

Color Scheme

The palette is inspired by the elegance of Nigerian festive attire.

#8B0000
#D4AF37
#FDFCF0

Deep Maroon (#8B0000): Used for primary branding, headers, and navigation.

Metallic Gold (#D4AF37): Used for call-to-action buttons, accents, and border highlights.

Cream (#FDFCF0): Used as the primary background color to maintain a soft, premium aesthetic.
