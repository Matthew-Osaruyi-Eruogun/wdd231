NP Membership Benefits

  • Basic directory listing.
  • Access to general chamber newsletters.

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • **All NP benefits included.**
  • Discounted admission to one special chamber event per year.
  • Access to member-only business resources portal.
×

Silver Membership Benefits

  • **All Bronze benefits included.**
  • 20% discount on all chamber event and training tickets.
  • One brief mention/feature in the quarterly e-newsletter.
  • Free annual business consultation session (1 hour).
×

Gold Membership Benefits

  • **All Silver benefits included.**
  • 50% discount on all chamber event and training tickets.
  • Quarterly 'Spotlight' advertising position on the Chamber home page.
  • Exclusive invitation to VIP networking events with board members.
