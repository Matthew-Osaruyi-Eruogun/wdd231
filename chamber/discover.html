Discover the Heart of Edo State: Benin City

Benin City is a historical and commercial hub, offering a unique blend of ancient heritage and modern commerce. Our city appeals to both businesses looking for strategic growth and visitors seeking a cultural experience.

Benin City Demographics & Features

Population (Metro): Approx. 2.5 Million

Major Industries: Manufacturing, Trade, IT/Tech, Agriculture

Key Feature: Home to the historic Kingdom of Benin.

Transportation: Major regional airport (BEN), extensive road network.
